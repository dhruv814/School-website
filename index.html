About us

Dharmajivan Higher Secondary School, managed by Saurastra Education Trust was established in the year 2001 with 287 students.The seed sown has become a great tree and has grown bigger and better since then. Today thousands and thousands of students are learning and growing under its shade.The dream which was visualized by the great persons like Shree Vallabhbhai Savani and educationist Shree Harshadbhai Rajguru, is taking its shape into reality.
